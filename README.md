# Blender-Project
A 20-second cinematic 3D animation created in Blender, featuring a suspenseful narrative between two characters. The scene is fully staged with custom environments, dynamic lighting, and scripted physics.

# Technical Specifications

  - Frame Rate: 24 FPS
  - Duration: 20+ Seconds
  - Software: Blender 4.x
  - Characters: Two characters sourced from Mixamo, including custom-retargeted animations.
  - Scripting: Custom Python script utilized to automate the physics and timing of the glass cup falling.

# The Storyline

The animation depicts a tense moment where a mysterious woman emerges from a wall, attempting to sneak into a young girl's bedroom while she is fast asleep. The suspense peaks when the girl suddenly wakes up, catching the intruder in the act. In her panic, the woman knocks over a glass cup on the bedside table. Startled by the noise, both characters freeze in shock before the woman retreats, sprinting back toward the wall to disappear.

# Features & Assets

  - Environment Design: A handcrafted bedroom environment including a detailed bed, mattress, side table, and the room structure itself.
  - Sound Design: Fully synchronized background audio, including:
      Sneaking/Footstep sound effects.
      High-tension shock cues.
      Glass shattering/falling sound.
      Running sound effects for the exit.
  - Lighting & Cinematography: Carefully adjusted lighting to enhance the "suspense/horror" atmosphere, with optimized camera angles to capture the emotional expressions of the characters.
